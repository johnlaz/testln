1 The flow

One button. Four invisible steps. Done.

Most notes apps make you do the filing. LazNote does it the moment you finish talking — so what's on your mind tonight is on the right stack by morning.

01 · Pulse

Hit the button

Long-press, talk, scribble, or paste. One target, anywhere in the app. No category to pick.

02 · Listen

Whisper transcribes

Your voice goes straight to Groq's Whisper. The transcript appears live as you speak.

03 · Sort

Llama files it

A 70b model reads context, your prior notes, and picks the right stack — or routes uncertain ones to the Airlock for a thumbs-up.

04 · Surface

Blade shows what burns

The home view is ranked by urgency, not by date created. NOW is bright. LATER fades. Stale notes ask to be archived.

2 What's inside

A notes app that thinks with you, not at you.

CAPTURE

Voice-first capture

Talk for 30 seconds, get 2 cards back. The AI segments rants into individual notes — separated, tagged, and filed.

BRAIN

Bring your own Groq

Paste a free Groq key, choose models per task. Your key, your tokens, your rate-limits. Nothing routed through us.

FOCUS

Blade prioritization

Notes ranked by urgency, not date. NOW notes pulse lime. SOON glows amber. IDLE fades back. The home view earns its real estate.

REVIEW

Airlock for uncertain notes

When the model isn't sure, the note sits in the Airlock with its reasoning. One tap to confirm. Zero notes get lost in the wrong stack.

CONTEXT

Why this stack?

Every AI decision is one tap from its receipt — the signals, the linked notes, the pattern. Disagree once and it learns.

OFFLINE

Local-first, install-anywhere

IndexedDB on every device. Works offline. Installs from any browser — iOS, Android, Mac, Windows, Linux. No App Store hoops.

3 Privacy by default

Your notes never touch our servers. Because there aren't any.

What stays here

  • Every note, ever. Stored in IndexedDB on this device only.
  • Your Groq API key. Lives in browser keychain, encrypted at rest.
  • Audio recordings, transcripts, AI reasoning — all local.
  • No accounts. No analytics. No telemetry. No third-party scripts.

What goes to Groq

  • Only when you Pulse: the note text + a list of your stack names.
  • Sent over HTTPS, directly from your browser to api.groq.com.
  • Groq's privacy policy applies to that single call — nothing else.
  • Want to use it offline? Manual filing still works. AI is optional.
