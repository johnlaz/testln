Help
How do I capture a note?
Tap the green Pulse button (●) at the bottom centre of the Blades screen. Type, record your voice, or scan text with your camera.
What is the Airlock?
When AI is unsure where to file a note (confidence <70%) it goes to the Airlock for your review. Tap a note to confirm or move it.
How do I sort with AI?
Add your Groq API key in Settings → Groq, then tap "Sort with AI" when capturing. AI assigns a stack, urgency, tags, and reasoning.
What are Stacks?
Stacks are folders — Biz, DIY, Dev, Personal by default. Create custom stacks in the Stacks tab. AI learns your stacks automatically.
How do I re-run AI on an existing note?
Open the note → tap Logic → tap "Re-sort with AI". This runs full AI classification on the current note text and updates stack, tags, and reasoning.
What do Summarize and Advice do?
Both are inside the Logic panel (requires Groq key). Summarize gives a 2–3 sentence capture of the note. Advice gives concrete action steps tailored to the note's stack.
How does voice recording work?
Tap the mic icon in capture mode → tap Start. Your recording is sent to Groq Whisper for transcription when you tap "Stop & use".
Is my data private?
Yes. Notes are stored only on this device in IndexedDB. Nothing is sent anywhere except your Groq API calls, which go directly to api.groq.com.
